Blue Prince Achievement Guide

Blue Prince achievements reward patient mapping, clue tracking, and flexible route planning. Treat each run as data collection first, then push for specific unlocks once your house knowledge is reliable.

Quick Answer

The best Blue Prince achievement strategy is to finish your first major objective without over-optimizing, then build a checklist around room discoveries, unusual interactions, resource milestones, and puzzle clues.

Achievement Route

  1. Play early runs for discovery instead of perfect routing.
  2. Record rooms, symbols, locked paths, and strange interactions.
  3. Separate story progress from optional challenge attempts.
  4. Use later runs to target one or two achievements at a time.
  5. Review missed conditions before restarting a promising run.

What To Track

Track room names, resource sources, locked doors, item interactions, and repeated clues. Many achievement runs fail because players know the answer once, but cannot recreate the route when the house changes.

Common Mistakes

  • Trying to force 100 percent before understanding room logic.
  • Restarting too quickly instead of learning from a failed draft.
  • Ignoring notes because the clue feels obvious in the moment.
  • Chasing too many achievements in one run.

FAQ

Should I use spoilers for Blue Prince achievements?

Use spoiler-free notes first. Look up exact hidden conditions only when discovery stops being fun.

Are failed runs wasted?

No. Failed runs are often the fastest way to learn room behavior and unlock conditions.

What should I do after finishing the main goal?

Start targeted cleanup: one route for room knowledge, one for resource tasks, and one for hidden interactions.

Is 100 percent hard?

It is more methodical than mechanical. Notes matter more than reflexes.

What is the best tool?

A simple room checklist is more useful than relying on memory.
