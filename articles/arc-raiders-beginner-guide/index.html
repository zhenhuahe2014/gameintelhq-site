Arc Raiders Beginner Guide

Arc Raiders becomes much easier when beginners stop treating each run like a normal shooter match and start treating extraction as the main objective.

Batch 006 Published May 31, 2026
Cover image placeholder

Quick Answer

Your first goal is not to win every fight. Learn safe routes, listen for threats, loot with a purpose, extract before greed takes over, and bring gear you can afford to lose.

First Priorities

  • Pick a simple loot goal before entering the map.
  • Learn one reliable extraction route before experimenting.
  • Leave early when you find something valuable.
  • Use sound cues to avoid fights you do not need.

Beginner Mistakes

  • Chasing every gunfight after finding good loot.
  • Bringing your best gear before learning map flow.
  • Ignoring audio because the screen looks quiet.
  • Splitting too far from teammates during extraction pressure.
Extraction route placeholder

Best Recommendation

Treat every successful extraction as progress, even if the loot is modest. Consistent survival teaches more than one lucky run with gear you cannot replace.

FAQ

Should beginners fight other players?

Fight when it protects your extraction or objective, not just because you heard shots.

What gear should I bring first?

Bring practical gear you can lose without ruining your next run.

Is Arc Raiders beginner friendly?

It can be, if you accept that learning routes and risk is part of the game.

Should I play solo or co-op?

Co-op is easier for learning, but solo helps you build stronger awareness.
