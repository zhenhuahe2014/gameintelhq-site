Beginner Guide

Where Winds Meet Beginner Guide

Start by learning the combat rhythm, unlocking flexible travel options, and upgrading the tools you actually use. Do not chase every side activity before your core build feels stable.

Quick Answer

New players should focus on one main weapon style, learn defensive timing, unlock exploration tools, and avoid spreading upgrades across too many systems. A narrow early build is easier to understand and stronger than a messy one.

Best First Priorities

  1. Pick one weapon style and practice its timing.
  2. Upgrade survivability before chasing damage only.
  3. Complete early activities that improve travel and recovery.
  4. Keep a simple set of skills you can use under pressure.
  5. Return to harder fights after you understand enemy patterns.

Combat Tips

Do not button-mash through early fights. Watch enemy timing, learn when to defend, and punish openings. Many new players lose because they attack constantly instead of letting the fight reveal a safe rhythm.

Common Mistakes

  • Upgrading every weapon at once.
  • Skipping defensive tools because damage looks more exciting.
  • Ignoring exploration rewards that make the map easier.
  • Fighting overleveled enemies too early.

FAQ

What should beginners upgrade first?

Upgrade the weapon and survival tools you use most often.

Is exploration important early?

Yes. Exploration often gives tools and resources that make combat easier.

Should I rush the main story?

Progress enough to unlock core systems, then slow down and build strength.

Can I change builds later?

Usually yes, but early focus prevents wasted time and confusion.

What is the best beginner habit?

Learn enemy patterns before spending resources trying to brute-force fights.
