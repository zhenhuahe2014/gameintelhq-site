Subnautica 2 Best Base Location

The best Subnautica 2 base location should shorten safe resource loops, stay easy to find, leave expansion room, and make deeper exploration less stressful.

Batch 006 Published May 31, 2026
Cover image placeholder

Quick Answer

Build your first serious base near recognizable landmarks, reliable early resources, manageable depth, and clear travel paths. Avoid locations that look dramatic but make every supply run slow or dangerous.

What Makes A Good Base Spot?

  • Easy navigation from your starting area and common routes.
  • Nearby access to basic crafting resources.
  • Enough flat or open space for expansion.
  • Safe oxygen timing for early-game travel.

What To Avoid

  • Deep locations before you have reliable travel tools.
  • Beautiful spots with poor resource access.
  • Tight spaces that make expansion awkward.
  • Areas that co-op teammates cannot find quickly.
Base placement placeholder

Best Recommendation

Use your first base for function. Once storage, crafting, and route planning feel stable, you can build a prettier outpost near deeper or more scenic areas.

FAQ

Should I build deep early?

No. Build where oxygen and return routes are easy until your tools improve.

Does co-op change base placement?

Yes. A shared base should be obvious, labeled, and easy for everyone to reach.

Should I move my first base?

Move or add an outpost when your main exploration routes shift farther away.

What matters more, safety or resources?

Early on, safety wins. A safe base keeps every mistake cheaper.
